Emotional Danger Zones

FOMO Trading

"Everyone's making money except me!"
Result: Chasing prices → Losses

Solution: Wait 10 minutes before entering

Revenge Trading

"I'll make back my losses now!"
Result: Bigger losses

Solution: Stop trading for the day

Overconfidence

"I'm on a winning streak!"
Result: Risking too much

Solution: Stick to position sizing

The Pro Trader Routine

1

Pre-Market Checklist

  • Review economic calendar
  • Set daily profit/loss limits
  • Choose 2-3 trading pairs
2

Trading Session Rules

  • Max 3 trades per day
  • Mandatory breaks every hour
  • No trading during news events
3

Post-Trading Review

  • Journal emotions felt
  • Analyze trade decisions
  • Plan next day
Discipline is like a muscle - it gets stronger with practice!

Mental Trading Tools

The 10-Minute Rule

When emotional:
1. Step away from charts
2. Breathe deeply for 2 minutes
3. Drink water
4. Return if calm

Trading Journal

Entry Example:
"Felt anxious after loss - took break instead of revenge trading"

Mental Rehearsal

Visualize successful trades nightly
Athletes use this technique!
