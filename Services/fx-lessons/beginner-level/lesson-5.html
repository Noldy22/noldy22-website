Lesson 5: Fundamental Analysis Basics

You'll Learn: How real-world events move currency values like weather affects crops

The Economic Weather Report

Simple Definition:

Fundamental analysis studies how countries' economic health affects their currency value - like checking a company's profits before buying its stock.

Country Comparison Analogy:

Strong Economy = Valuable Currency
(Like a profitable company's stock)

Central Banks = Currency Gardeners
(They 'water' economy with interest rates)

Economic Health Metrics

GDP (Gross Domestic Product)

What it Measures: Total value of goods/services produced

Example Impact:
US GDP grows 3% → USD rises

Inflation (CPI)

What it Measures: Price increases over time

Example Impact:
Eurozone CPI at 5% → ECB may raise rates

Employment Data

What it Measures: Job market health

Example Impact:
Canada adds 100K jobs → CAD strengthens

The Interest Rate Gardeners

US Federal Reserve (Fed)

Current Rate: 5.50%
Last Change: +0.25% (July 2023)

European Central Bank (ECB)

Current Rate: 4.25%

Interest Rate Effects

Rate Increase

Currency Usually Rises
Like better savings account rates

Rate Decrease

Currency Usually Falls
Like store having a clearance sale

Market-Moving Events

2020

COVID-19 Pandemic

USD surged 10% as investors sought safety

2016

Brexit Vote

GBP fell 15% against USD in 2 days

2022

Russia-Ukraine War

EUR fell 5% vs CHF in 1 week

Check economic calendars! Major events cause currency storms

Practice Analysis

Hypothetical Scenario:

The Fed warns of future rate hikes while EU unemployment rises.
What happens to EUR/USD?

Higher US rates attract investors → USD demand increases
EU economic weakness → EUR becomes less attractive
