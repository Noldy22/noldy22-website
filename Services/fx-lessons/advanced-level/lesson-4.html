Professional Risk Management

You'll Master: Advanced position sizing models and correlation risk analysis

What is Professional Risk Management?

Protecting Capital Like a Pro:

Professional risk management goes beyond basic stop losses. It uses advanced models to control exposure, manage portfolio risk, and adapt to changing market correlations.

  • Position Sizing Models: Calculate optimal trade size for each setup
  • Correlation Risk: Manage exposure across related assets
  • Portfolio Approach: View risk at the account level, not just per trade

Advanced Position Sizing Models

Precision in Every Trade:

Position sizing determines how much to risk on each trade. Advanced models like Kelly Criterion, fixed fractional, and volatility-based sizing help maximize growth while controlling drawdowns.

  • Fixed Fractional: Risk a set % of equity per trade
  • Volatility-Based: Adjust size based on market volatility (e.g., ATR)
  • Kelly Criterion: Optimize size for long-term growth

Correlation Risk Analysis

Don't Double Your Risk:

Assets can move together. Correlation risk means losses can multiply if you’re exposed to similar trades. Analyze correlations to diversify and avoid concentration risk.

  • Asset Correlations: Identify pairs that move together
  • Diversification: Spread risk across uncorrelated assets
  • Hidden Exposure: Watch for indirect correlations in your portfolio

Key Risk Management Rules

Stay in the Game:

Follow strict rules to protect your capital and survive market swings.

  • Max Risk per Trade: Usually 1-2% of account equity
  • Max Open Risk: Limit total risk across all trades
  • Correlation Cap: Avoid overexposure to correlated assets
  • Dynamic Sizing: Adjust for volatility and news
